Safety & Privacy

Privacy & Conservation

1. Location Data: We use your GPS to help Rangers find injured wildlife. Your exact location is never shown to other users.

2. Fuzzy Maps: Public sightings are obscured by a 50-100m "blur" to prevent deer harassment.

3. Anonymous: No personal identity or contact info is stored. Reports are tied to anonymous sessions.
